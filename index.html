Hyeokjae Kwon, M.D., Ph.D.

Surgeon & Computer Scientist
  • Visiting Professor, Massachusetts General Hospital (MGH), USA (2026.03 ~ Present)
  • Clinical Assistant Professor, Dept. of Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery, Chungnam National University Hospital

Dr. Hyeokjae Kwon holds a unique interdisciplinary background with a B.S. in Computer Science from KAIST and an M.D., Ph.D. in Medicine from Chungnam National University. His research integrates artificial intelligence, medical computer vision, and virtual reality preoperative education into plastic and reconstructive surgery, with dedicated research interests in chronic wounds and surgical site infections (SSI).
