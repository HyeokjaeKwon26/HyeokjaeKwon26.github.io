Interdisciplinary Research

Core Research Focus Areas

Integrating computational models, deep learning vision, and virtual reality into surgical practice.

AI & Deep Learning Medical Vision

Developing deep learning frameworks for automated facial parameter assessment, infrared image analysis for MRD-1 measurement, and AI-driven eyeball exposure rate (EER) evaluation.

VR & LiDAR Medical Sensors

Pioneering mobile LiDAR application for precise real-time wound size measurement and immersive Virtual Reality (VR) preoperative education reducing patient anxiety.

Microvascular Reconstructive Surgery

Customized lower eyelid reconstruction, thin thoracodorsal artery perforator (TDAP) flaps, turbocharging anterolateral thigh (ALT) free flaps, and flap perfusion monitoring.

Aesthetic & Facial Trauma Surgery

Minimally invasive facial granuloma removal via pretrichial approach, midface lifting, and extended transconjunctival open reduction of complex zygoma fractures.
