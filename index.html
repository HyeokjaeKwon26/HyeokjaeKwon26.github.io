Surgeon & Computer Scientist
  • Visiting Professor, Massachusetts General Hospital (MGH), USA (2026.03 ~ Present)
  • Clinical Assistant Professor, Dept. of Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery, Chungnam National University Hospital

Dr. Hyeokjae Kwon holds a unique interdisciplinary background with a B.S. in Computer Science from KAIST and an M.D., Ph.D. in Medicine from Chungnam National University. His research integrates artificial intelligence, medical computer vision, virtual reality preoperative education, and smartphone-based LiDAR sensing into plastic and reconstructive microvascular surgery.
